About Me

Bharadwaj is a talented robotics engineer from the University of Maryland. I have a strong background in designing and building semi-autonomous machines from my previous work experience. I am passionate about developing robot software and building autonomous robots. Currently working on a project to build a robot that can navigate autonomously in a dynamic environment. I am skilled in programming languages such as C++, Python, and object-oriented programming, as well as software engineering, machine learning, and simulation software. I am also skilled in computer vision, image processing, and statistical pattern recognition. I am a quick learner and a team player. I am looking for a challenging position in robotics engineering. I am excited to use my skills and experience to develop innovative robotics solutions that can improve the lives of people and communities. I am set to graduate in May 2023 and am eager to begin my career as a robotics engineer. I am confident that my skills and passion for robotics make me an excellent candidate for any opportunity in the field.
